Scoban Pham, Brianna Nguyen

2021-12-07

How to play

When the project is first launched, the user will see a Main Menu screen, where they are instructed to press the SPACE bar to begin the Snake Game.

The game takes place on a square grid where the Snake is constantly moving, and its direction can be controlled by the player using arrow keys. The objective of the game is to collect “food” squares that randomly appear on the grid. This will increase the size of the snake as well as the player’s score. The player must also prevent the snake from running into itself or into the outer boundaries of the grid, or else it is game over.

When the game ends, the player will be met with a Game Over screen, where they are able to see their final score. The player is also given the option to restart the game and play again by pressing the SPACE bar.
